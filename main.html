Goa

This place is known for its famous beaches, culture, climate, and historical places. You may find colourful architecture, lush greenery, and breathtaking beaches here. Goa is a perfect blend of Portugal and Indian culture. People get attracted to this place to experience world-class music, dance, and sunset.

Top 5 attractions
  1. Varca Beach - Great beach with entertainment and water sports.
  2. Big Daddy casino - the Best casino in Goa, offering the ultimate gaming experience!
  3. Reis magos fort - The place are wonderful to visit
  4. Boat cruising - Embrace luxury with this yacht tour of Goa
  5. Portugal trails - Traces of Portuguese heritage and history are rife throughout Goa
Must-try foods
  • Chicken cafreal - Cafreal chicken is a spicy green coloured dish that will leave your mouth watering
  • Shark Ambot tik - Ambot Tik is a Portuguese influenced Goan food.
  • Sorak - Sorak is a simple vegetarian curry made especially during the monsoon season in Goa.
